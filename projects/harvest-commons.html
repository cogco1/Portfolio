STUDIO WORK 课程设计

Harvest Commons

Multi-generation living and self-sufficient agriculture. · 多代际共居与自给农业。

Year 时间2026 Spring 春
Type 类型Multi-generation housing · agriculture 多代际住宅 · 自给农业
Role 角色Group · with Xinyue Liang 小组
Professor 指导Leonard Leung
Awards 奖项Michael Hollander Honorable Mention · Studio Archive Prize
Status 状态Completed 已完成

Harvest Commons is a multi-generation housing study that pairs collective living with self-sufficient agriculture. Productive landscape — gardens, terraces, and shared growing space — is woven through the building so that food, care, and daily life are organized together rather than apart.

Harvest Commons 是一项将集体居住与自给农业结合的多代际住宅研究。把可生产的景观——菜园、台地与共享种植空间——编织进建筑，使食物、照护与日常生活被一并组织，而非彼此割裂。

Private units are layered around shared cores and growing terraces, letting different generations live independently while sharing the work and the yield of the land. · 私人单元层叠环绕共享核心与种植台地，让不同世代既能独立生活，又共同分担土地的劳作与收成。

A group project developed with Xinyue Liang. · 与 Xinyue Liang 合作完成的小组项目。
