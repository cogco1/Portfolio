Architectural designer · Explainable-systems researcher · Builder

I design buildings and build the systems that reason about them. My work began with large-scale Minecraft worlds—now a community of 150K+ followers and 3M+ downloads—and extends through architectural studios, professional workflows, and ArchFlow, a white-box framework for auditable architectural generation. Across these scales, I pursue one question: how can spatial intent remain legible as design becomes computational?

建筑设计 · 可解释系统研究 · 数字建造

我既设计建筑，也构建能够对建筑作出推理的系统。我的实践始于大尺度 Minecraft 世界，并发展为拥有 15 万余名关注者、累计下载超过 300 万次的创作社群；如今，它延伸到建筑设计课、专业生产流程，以及白盒建筑生成框架 ArchFlow。贯穿这些尺度的问题始终是：当设计进入计算过程，空间意图如何继续保持可读、可验与可修订？

Selected Research & Development

2026 – Present

ArchFlow — Explainable Architectural Generation System

Founder & Lead Developer

  • Building an intent-to-architecture compiler in which briefs become constrained decisions, obligations, geometry, and repairable provenance.
  • Separating legality, function, and taste: deterministic masks and validators establish validity; causal traces preserve design reasons; critics rank compliant alternatives.
  • Using voxel architecture as a low-cost experimental space for false-negative probes, selective repair, and reproducible evaluation—not as the final product category.
