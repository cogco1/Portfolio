STUDIO WORK · DESIGN-LED COLLABORATION

Harvest Commons

Multi-generational living and self-sufficient agriculture.多代际共居与自给农业。

YearSpring 2026
TypeMulti-generational housing · agriculture
RoleDesign lead · collaborative
CollaboratorXinyue Liang
ProfessorLeonard Leung
RecognitionMichael Hollander Honorable Mention · Studio Archive Prize
StatusCompleted
AUTHORSHIP

Design lead. Kaiwen led the overall architectural direction and principal design decisions; the project was developed collaboratively with Xinyue Liang.

设计主导。刘铠闻主导整体建筑方向与核心设计判断，项目与 Xinyue Liang 合作完成。

Harvest Commons is a multi-generational housing study that pairs collective living with self-sufficient agriculture. Productive landscape — gardens, terraces, and shared growing space — is woven through the building so that food, care, and daily life are organised together rather than apart.

Harvest Commons 是一项将集体居住与自给农业结合的多代际住宅研究。把可生产的景观——菜园、台地与共享种植空间——编织进建筑，使食物、照护与日常生活被一并组织，而非彼此割裂。

Private units are layered around shared cores and growing terraces, letting different generations live independently while sharing the work and the yield of the land.

私人单元层叠环绕共享核心与种植台地，让不同世代既能独立生活，又共同分担土地的劳作与收成。
